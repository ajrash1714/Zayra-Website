AI-Governed Cardiac Intelligence

Predictive ECG skin patch to prevent sudden cardiac deaths.

Zayra is a patent-backed AI wearable that continuously listens to the heart, detects dangerous patterns early, and alerts clinicians before catastrophe.

Built in India by founder Roslyn Singh Ahluwalia, Zayra India Pvt Ltd is creating a new category of cardiac intelligence – combining medical‑grade ECG sensing, flexible electronics, and cloud AI.

The Zayra system

Patent-backed ECG skin patch

Zayra’s wearable uses a biocompatible ECG skin patch with flexible printed electronics and conductive ink to capture high-fidelity cardiac signals continuously, without disrupting daily life.

The patch integrates electrodes, a microcontroller, wireless communication, and on-board preprocessing optimised for low noise and reliable clinical data.

Cloud AI cardiac intelligence

ECG data flows securely from the patch to the cloud, where AI models perform arrhythmia detection, HRV analysis, and early warning for high-risk cardiac events.

Clinicians receive structured insights rather than raw waveforms, helping them intervene early for the right patient at the right time.

Beyond a device

Continuous cardiac intelligence network

Every heartbeat becomes structured data feeding a living cardiac intelligence network.

Zayra is designed as a longitudinal platform, enabling population‑scale models that can spot subtle deterioration, risky rhythms, and patterns that precede myocardial infarction or sudden cardiac arrest.
