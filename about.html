About Me

I am a healthcare founder, mother of two, and former government school teacher who chose to solve one of the hardest problems in cardiology: catching life‑threatening events before they strike.

After a decade inside India’s public system, I saw how late diagnosis, limited monitoring, and unequal access leave families exposed to preventable cardiac tragedies. Zayra was born from that lived experience – a decision to build an AI‑enabled ECG skin patch that can live quietly on the chest, continuously listen to the heart, and raise the alarm when it truly matters.

As Founder & CEO of Zayra India Pvt Ltd, I am leading the journey from patent to product: integrating medical‑grade ECG sensing, flexible electronics, cloud AI, and clinical workflows into a single, elegant wearable that fits into daily life.

Vision for Zayra

From device to intelligence network

Zayra is not just a patch – it is the foundation of a continuous cardiac intelligence network. Each heartbeat becomes structured data, feeding AI models that can detect subtle deterioration, dangerous arrhythmias, and early warning signs of myocardial infarction.

My vision is a world where every high‑risk patient, every cardiac survivor, and every silent high‑risk profile can be protected by an invisible safety net of predictive monitoring – starting in India and scaling globally.

Building a global healthtech brand

I am building Zayra as a clinically serious, design‑obsessed, and globally relevant company. That means deep partnerships with cardiologists and hospitals, regulatory‑grade science, and a product experience that feels as refined as the world’s best consumer technology.

The ambition is clear: Zayra should stand among the most valuable healthtech brands of this decade, measured not only in valuation, but in the number of sudden cardiac deaths we help prevent.
