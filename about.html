Aditya Raj

Founder & CEO, Zayra

Driving Innovation in Cardiac Care

Aditya Raj is a visionary healthcare technology entrepreneur focused on democratizing cardiac health monitoring through AI-powered wearable solutions. With deep expertise in machine learning, ECG signal processing, and medical device development, Aditya founded Zayra with a mission to make advanced cardiac diagnostics accessible to everyone.

Background: A skilled ML engineer with experience in deep learning CNN architectures, Aditya has trained models on clinical ECG datasets (PTB-XL) achieving state-of-the-art performance. His technical foundation combines signal processing expertise with real-world medical device validation.

Vision: To bridge the gap between hospital-grade diagnostics and personal health monitoring, enabling early detection of cardiac anomalies and saving lives through continuous, non-invasive monitoring.
