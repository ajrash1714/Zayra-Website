The Mission

Heart disease is the silent killer — often striking without warning. Traditional methods rely on intermittent snapshots, missing the crucial early signals that precede cardiac events.

Zayra's AI-powered patch changes that paradigm entirely. By continuously learning your heart's unique electrical signature, it predicts risk trajectories before they become crises. This isn't just monitoring — it's prevention reimagined.

Every heartbeat tells a story. Zayra listens, learns, and protects.

Why Zayra

India faces a cardiac epidemic. Yet access to continuous, intelligent monitoring remains a luxury. Zayra breaks that barrier — delivering hospital-grade intelligence in a patch you wear.

Built for extreme environments, from high-altitude military operations to intensive industrial work, Zayra doesn't compromise. It's ruggedized, medical-grade, and powered by AI trained on millions of cardiac cycles.

This is cardiac intelligence, democratized.
